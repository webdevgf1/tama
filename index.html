About Tamaweb P2E

Tamaweb is a revolutionary play-to-earn virtual pet game built on the Solana blockchain. Earn $TAMA tokens while caring for your digital companions in this next-generation Web3 gaming experience.

Key Features

  • Earn $TAMA tokens through pet care activities
  • Built on Solana for fast, low-cost transactions
  • True ownership of virtual pets as NFTs
  • Trade tokens on decentralized exchanges
  • Stake tokens for additional rewards
